Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-14

Lightweight is a workout tracking application operated by Lewis Thompson (operating as 3rigby). This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account credentials — username and a hashed password, created when you register
  • Workout data — exercises, sets, reps, weights, and session history that you log in the app

We do not automatically collect crash reports, analytics, device identifiers, IP addresses, location data, or any diagnostic data. All data in Lightweight is explicitly provided by you.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Lightweight service: storing your workouts, tracking your progression, and syncing between devices. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored on a server operated by 3rigby, hosted by DigitalOcean in their Sydney, Australia region. Data is not transferred to any other third party.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or external integrations in the app.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below. Deletion requests will be processed within 30 days.

Security

Passwords are hashed before storage. All communication between the app and server is encrypted via HTTPS. Access to the server is restricted to the application operator.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Children's Privacy

Lightweight is not directed at children. You must be at least 13 years old to create an account. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has created an account, we will delete it and all associated data promptly.
